Kharagpur-the mini India

Kharagpur is an upcoming fourth largest city in WestBengal state.It is located under district Paschim Medinipur. A city having lush greenery with a touch of ethnic multiculture and secular in its true form.It has one of the largest railway workshops in India, and the third longest railway platform in the world (1072.5m).It covers an area of 127 square kilometers with an elevation of 61 mts from sea level. As per the recent 2020 census population of kharagpur is approximately three lakhs fifty thousand with an influx of majority coming from neighbouring states for livelyhood. A large herd of different community has grown here to a substantial amount because of one of the premium institute of our country, viz IIT. Here techngrads flocks in large amount to pursue their dream of becoming engineers of next generation. Kharagpur is located at WikiMiniAtlas 22.330239°N 87.323653°E longitude and latitude respectively.
